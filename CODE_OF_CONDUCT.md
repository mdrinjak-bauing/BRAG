# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This is a small, friendly open-source project. Everyone interacting in the
issues, pull requests, and discussions is expected to be respectful, patient,
and constructive — regardless of background or experience level.

## In short

- Be kind and assume good intent.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Help newcomers; we all started somewhere.

This project follows the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact the maintainer
privately at **markodri92@gmail.com**. Reports are handled discreetly and in
good faith.
